INDUSTRY PROJECT

College Admission Form and Management System

Description:

Designed and developed a college admission form and management system using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL. The system allows students to fill out their personal and academic details, and the information is stored in a secure database. The system includes a dashboard that requires a login for access and is only accessible by the manager and heads of eight different departments. The manager can view and manage student data, while the heads of each department can view the details of students who have been allocated to their respective departments. FPDF was used to generate PDF files and provide additional features for the heads of departments.
